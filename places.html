Siddheshwar Temple

Located near Siddheshwar Lake, this temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva. Thousands of devotees visit daily, especially during Siddheshwar Yatra festival.

Pandharpur

A sacred town 70 km from Solapur, famous for Vitthal Rukmini Temple. Major festivals include Ashadhi and Kartiki Ekadashi.

Akkalkot

Located 40 km away, known for Shri Swami Samarth Temple. A peaceful spiritual destination.

Solapur Fort

Also called Bhuikot Fort, built during Bahmani Sultanate. It reflects medieval military architecture.
